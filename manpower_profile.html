Manpower Profile
Shreeji Construction is a Bhavnagar, Gujarat-based industrial manpower and structural engineering firm established in 2007. We specialize in supplying compliant, safety-inducted skilled and unskilled workforce for industrial operations, plant maintenance, and structural fabrication projects across India.
Who We Are
Industrial Manpower, Delivered with Precision.

For over 15 years, Shreeji Construction has been the trusted manpower partner of choice for industrial clients across Gujarat and pan-India. We provide end-to-end workforce solutions — from sourcing and vetting to deployment and statutory compliance — ensuring every professional we place is safety-inducted, legally protected, and operationally ready from day one.

All personnel undergo mandatory EHS induction, tool-box talks (TBT), and Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment (HIRA) before mobilization. Our operations comply with the Factories Act, 1948 and BOCW Act, 1996.
