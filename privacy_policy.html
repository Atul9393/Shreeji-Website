Corporate Integrity

Privacy Policy

Shreeji Construction is committed to maintaining the confidentiality, security, and integrity of all client and operational data.

1. Data Collection

We collect information that you explicitly submit through our **Project Inquiry Form** (Contact page) and **Schedule Consultation** triggers. This data includes your full name, work email address, phone number, company name, project type, and any detailed message regarding your project requirements.

2. Purpose & Data Usage

All collected information is used strictly and exclusively for professional business communications. This includes providing project estimates, scheduling technical consultations, verifying staffing requirements, and fulfilling our service delivery obligations. We do **not** sell, trade, or lease your corporate or personal data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Information Confidentiality

As a partner in heavy fabrication and manpower supply, we understand the critical and sensitive nature of industrial site schedules and operations. We maintain strict access control protocols for shutdown timelines, blueprint specifications, and deployed personnel registers. Data is restricted to authorized operations managers to prevent disclosure.

4. Statutory Compliance

Our privacy practices conform to the Information Technology Act, 2000 and consumer data rules in India.
