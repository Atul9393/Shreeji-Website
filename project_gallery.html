Project 04 High-Altitude Erection Crane Rigging

High-Altitude Structural Erection — Multi-Storey Industrial Columns

Gujarat · Advanced Crane Rigging & Structural Erection

person_raised_handThe Client Need

An industrial plant expansion required erection of multi-storey structural steel columns and beams at heights exceeding 40 metres. The primary client requirement was a contractor with proven high-altitude erection capability, crane rigging competency, and an unimpeachable safety record for working-at-height tasks.

warningThe Challenge
  • arrow_rightAchieving plumb and level tolerances on tall columns where minor base deviations are magnified significantly at top elevation.
  • arrow_rightCrane positioning constrained by existing plant infrastructure — required detailed lift planning and ground-bearing assessment.
  • arrow_rightWorking-at-height risk at 40+ metres required mandatory harness, rescue plan, and exclusion zone protocols enforced without exception.
engineeringOur Solution
  • check_circlePrepared a formal Lift Plan and Rigging Study reviewed by a certified rigging engineer prior to any crane mobilisation.
  • check_circleInstalled temporary bracing after each column erection and performed theodolite plumb checks before proceeding to the next lift.
  • check_circlePre-task HIRA conducted before every shift; full-body harness and rescue-at-height equipment inspected daily by safety supervisor.
