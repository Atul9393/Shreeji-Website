Our Vetting Standard

We follow a 4-stage technical verification process to ensure every worker exceeds industry benchmarks before they reach your site.

01 / Screening

Credential Audit

Rigorous verification of ITI certificates, previous experience letters, and 6G testing records.

02 / Testing

Skill Assessment

Practical workshop testing including coupon welding and blueprint reading evaluations.

03 / Medical

04 / Induction

Mandatory EHS induction and specialized tool-handling orientation before mobilization.
